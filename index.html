Nixon: Slow Violence

By slow violence I mean a violence that occurs gradually and out of sight, a violence of delayed destruction that is dispersed across time and space, an attritional violence that is typically not viewed as violence at all. Violence is customarily concieved as an event or action that is immediate in time, explosive and spectacular in space, and as erupting into sensational visibility. We need, I believe, to engage a different kind of violence (2)
